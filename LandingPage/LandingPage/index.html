Why We Built Gaza Triage App

In Gaza, victims often die before reaching hospitals. Roads are destroyed, and the internet is down. Gaza Triage App equips civilians and medics to respond instantly: offline, bilingual, and fast.

It reflects our humanitarian, technical, and spiritual duty to protect life in the Ummah. Every feature is designed with one goal: saving lives when traditional systems fail.
